We Reach
Celebrating the decades of images from the Hubble and James Webb telescopes, the poetry (by F. Taylor Atkinson) delves deep into humanity's unyielding quest to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos. Through soaring melodies and intricate harmonies, the piece paints a vivid portrait of our collective pursuit of understanding, punctuated by moments of triumph and revelation.
