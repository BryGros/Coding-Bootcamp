About Me

My name is Bry-guy-so-fly, and I believe everyone— especially musicians— deserves a clear, confidence-building financial education. I created Gig to Gold to demystify money with practical, no-jargon guidance you can use between rehearsals and gigs: simple lessons, step-by-step plans, and tools that turn small, steady actions into long-term results. Whether you’re just starting to get paid for your art or planning your next big move, my goal is to help you keep more of what you earn and grow it—so your talent funds the life you want.
